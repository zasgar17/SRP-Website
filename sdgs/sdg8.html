Ending poverty in all its forms remains one of the greatest challenges facing humanity. Despite progress, millions still live in extreme poverty. Sustainable economic growth, social protection, and equal opportunities are vital for addressing this global issue.

Decent Work and Economic Growth

Poverty affects access to food, education, healthcare, and basic human rights. Eradicating it requires policies that ensure economic resources and services for all, with a focus on marginalized communities. By supporting inclusive development and fair wages, we can build a more equitable world.
