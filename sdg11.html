Sustainable Cities and Communities

Sustainable Development Goal 11 (SDG 11) seeks to make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable. As urban areas grow, challenges like overcrowding, pollution, and inadequate infrastructure arise. SDG 11 targets include ensuring access to affordable housing, sustainable transport, green public spaces, and disaster-resilient buildings. It also emphasizes participatory urban planning and reducing the environmental impact of cities. Sustainable cities foster better quality of life, economic opportunity, and environmental stewardship. By designing inclusive and forward-looking urban spaces, we create vibrant communities for current and future generations.
